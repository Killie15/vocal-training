Tuner Controls

Click the start button to activate the microphone. Hold your instrument close to the microphone and pluck a single string. The gauge displays cents flat (left) or sharp (right).

Strum a full chord on your guitar, ukulele, or piano. The analyzer maps frequency peaks across octaves to identify the chord type (Major, Minor, 7th) and lists all active notes.

Standard Instrument Reference:

  • Guitar (Standard): E2 (82Hz), A2 (110Hz), D3 (146Hz), G3 (196Hz), B3 (247Hz), E4 (329Hz)
  • Ukulele (Standard): G4 (392Hz), C4 (261Hz), E4 (329Hz), A4 (440Hz)
  • Violin (Standard): G3 (196Hz), D4 (293Hz), A4 (440Hz), E5 (659Hz)
